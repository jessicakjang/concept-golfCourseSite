  • Credit card only or pay ahead online.
  • Range reduced to 50% capacity.
  • Cups set to reduce touch points. Flags remain in the holes.

Course Information

Our parkland course features native trees and lush grass. With spectacular landscape views, this course perfectly encapsulates the region. Egret River is laid over 180 acres with some of the area's best greens.

Holes: 18 | Par: 72 | Yardage: 7,245
